Default Plugins

BrainStim supports the use of plugins to extend its functionality. The plugins may extend the internal Script engine (Script plugins) or the QML/QtQuick2 engine (QML plugins). Some of them are by default installed and directly available by BrainStim.

The script can be extended by two type of plugins, namely Device or Extension plugins. If your planning to extend BrainStim's functionality by writing your custom Script Plugin you might want to take a look at the ExtensionPlugin Template documentation which explains how to get started writing your own custom BrainStim Script Plug-in.
